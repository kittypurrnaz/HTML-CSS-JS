Founded in March 2017, Tiny Hotel is the first company in Australia to integrate the concept of “tiny hotels” with eco- tourism. “Tiny hotels” are part of an international trend of simplifying lodging by reducing the size, and therefore the resource consumption, of homes without sacrificing quality.

Tiny Hotel takes this one step further by placing its beautifully designed, compact homes on wheels in spectacular rural settings – amplifying the benefits of the “tiny house” movement by pairing the advantages of a thoughtfully efficient abode with the recharging

The brainchild of founders, Shawn and Susan wallers, Tiny Hotel stems from a desire to offer city dwellers a chance to experience the perfect escape from a hectic, digitally-laden lifestyle and enable anyone from around the world to have a part in this new way of tourism.
